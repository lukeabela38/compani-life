About

We're only starting our journey, and we've got a long way to go...

  • Saturday 16th August 2025

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Compani™ started as a small idea among a group of friends who simply wanted to bring more joy and connection into people’s lives. From these humble beginnings, we’ve grown into a disruptive force, transforming the way the world experiences companionship.

  • Wednesday 20th August 2025

    An Agency is Born

    From a spark of creativity and a shared passion for human connection, an agency is born—Compani™. What started as a playful experiment has evolved into a full-fledged platform redefining companionship in the modern era.

  • Thursday 21st August 2025

    Transition to Full Service

    What began as a boutique, bespoke experience has now transitioned into a full-service platform. Compani™ offers scalable, on-demand companionship solutions that cater to every need, mood, and moment.

  • Friday 22nd August 2025

    Phase Two Expansion

    With Phase Two Expansion, Compani™ is scaling globally, bringing our innovative companionship model to new markets. We’re leveraging technology, data, and a world-class team to deliver hyper-personalized experiences at an unprecedented scale.
